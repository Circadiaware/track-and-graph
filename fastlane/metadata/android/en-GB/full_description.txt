Track & Graph is a powerful tool for helping you track and analyse your habits, behaviours, and symptoms. Assign numerical values to track changes over time, or simply record when something happened. With Track & Graph, you can quickly and easily track everything in seconds and create insightful graphs to visualise your data. Set up line graphs, pie charts, and statistics to report useful information like moving averages and time between tracking. Organise your data into groups and arrange them however you like with ease. Track & Graph is completely free, open source, and has no authentication, ads, or in-app purchases. Your personal information, tracked data, and app activity are not recorded or stored online, and you can easily backup your data at any time. Enhance your self-awareness and facilitate self-reflection with Track & Graph.
